The mechanism

One rule, seven products

Every Trustora policy works the same way underneath - only the meter changes. Rainfall in centimetres, delay in minutes, a hailstorm flag, a lost-parcel status. Pick your meter, pick your line, get paid the second it's crossed.

01

Choose your meter

Rainfall for a field, delay minutes for a flight or train, a damage/loss flag for a parcel or bag.

02

Set your trigger

See the exact line on the gauge - the reading that turns your policy into a payout.

03

Pay the premium

Priced per acre, per vehicle, per trip or per parcel - shown before you commit.

04

Get paid automatically

The moment the public data feed reports a reading past your line, payout is released - no form to fill.
